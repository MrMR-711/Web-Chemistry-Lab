About Us

This web application project is designed to create an interactive and engaging space for learning and exploring the world of chemical elements. In this virtual lab, we have tried to present the complex concepts related to the atomic structure of elements in a simple and visual language

In the Elements Lab, you can easily change the number of protons, neutrons, and electrons of each element and witness the transformation of that element into another. This interactive approach helps you gain a deeper understanding of the periodic table and the relationship between subatomic particles and the identity of elements. Imagine, with just a few clicks, you can visually see how changing the number of protons not only changes the charge of the nucleus but also completely alters the nature of the element

This project is the result of the effort and passion of two chemistry enthusiasts, Mohammad Rashnou and Hamidreza Sepahvand. We have created this virtual lab with the aim of facilitating the learning of chemistry concepts for students and science enthusiasts. We hope that the Elements Lab can open a new window to the fascinating and complex world of elements for you and provide you with an enjoyable and educational experience

Thank you for choosing the Elements Lab to explore the world of elements! For more information and to view the codes, you can visit Mohammad Rashnou's GitHub repository
